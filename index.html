Welcome to JLN Public School

About Our School

Our school is an English medium school since 2004.

At JLN Public School, we focus on academic excellence, creative development, and social responsibility. Our trained educators provide a nurturing and safe environment where students thrive.

Special Facilities

AI & Robotics Education

JLN Public School is the first school in Etah to introduce AI-based learning! We offer **Neural Voice Text-to-Speech technology in 26 languages** for better learning. Students from **grades 6 to 8** learn **Arduino and Raspberry Pi programming** to develop technical skills. Our focus is on **Activity-Based Learning**, ensuring that children learn through interactive experiences. The campus is fully secured with **CCTV surveillance**, ensuring a safe environment for all students.
